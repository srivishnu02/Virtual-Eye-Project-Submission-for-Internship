About

"The custom dataset trained Yolov3 model is designed to classify between active drowning and swimming activities. It has been trained on a diverse range of images and videos featuring individuals engaged in these two distinct activities in various environments such as pools, lakes, and beaches. The model has undergone extensive training and fine-tuning to achieve high accuracy and reliable performance."
